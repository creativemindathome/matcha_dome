— HOW WE READ IT

The visible–NIR spectrum is a fingerprint. Water, chlorophyll, catechins, L-theanine and caffeine each absorb at their own wavelengths. We model the full 900–1650 nm curve against 12,000 farm-verified samples.

Each bowl is private. Spectra stay on-device until you choose to pair them with a tin.
