# 01 · Brief

## What we're building

The **Platôme Matcha** landing site — Phase 1 of the Platôme consumer platform. A single-page preorder site for:

1. **The Matcha Dome** — a chawan-sized NIR spectroscopy device that authenticates and grades matcha at the point of preparation.
2. **Platôme Matcha Subscription** — ceremonial-grade matcha shipped monthly, each tin with a spectral signature registered to the device.

These are sold together as a bundled preorder. The subscription absorbs into the full Platôme consumer platform at Phase 2 dome launch.

## Success criteria

- Live under `platome.com/matcha` (or standalone `platomematcha.com`) within 10 days.
- Converts ≥8% of visitors to email capture; ≥1.5% to €149 preorder deposit.
- Zero external raise messaging; bootstrap-feel, craft-feel, not startup-feel.
- Credible enough for premium matcha buyers (current alternative: Ippodo, Marukyu-Koyamaen) and for instrument-curious early adopters (current reference: Peak Design, Teenage Engineering, Kinto).

## Brand anchor

**Tagline**: *Measure the leaf, not the label.* (Pick during build if you prefer an alternative: *Authenticate every bowl*, or *A spectrometer for the tea ceremony*.)

**Positioning sentence**: Platôme Matcha is a ceremonial instrument that reads the near-infrared signature of your matcha and tells you what's actually in the bowl — grade, freshness, origin, theanine. Built for people who care about what they drink.

**Voice guidance**: write like Kinfolk, not like a DTC brand. No "revolutionize". No "game-changer". No exclamation marks. Sentences carry weight. Whitespace does work.

## Out of scope for this MVP

- Pharma / clinical messaging.
- Full Platôme dome product (the non-matcha-specific device is Phase 2).
- Dashboards, user accounts, app screens (beyond one mockup shot).
- Blog / content marketing.
- Multiple languages (English only).

## Reference aesthetics

Three references, each contributing a specific element:

1. **Whisk / Sans Brothers** — the editorial use of oversized numerals and layered typography. Borrowed sparingly in the Koku Score section.
2. **Idle Hour Matcha** — paper-dominant restraint, centered serif editorial body copy, three-image category gallery, newsletter footer. The overall site palette follows this reference most closely.
3. **Production Evolved (Spaceship-style Framer template)** — cinematic single-image hero with floating feature cards at the bottom. The structural model for Section 2.

Secondary vibe references (not copied, just informing the voice):
- **Kinto Japan** product pages — generous whitespace, slow pace, material honesty.
- **Teenage Engineering** OP-1 marketing — precision instrument aesthetics, dense spec tables in mono.
- **Aesop** product pages — editorial discipline, earned superlatives only.
- **Leica Q-series** — material reverence for a single object.

## Deliverables this site must produce

1. One landing page, long-scroll, 11 sections.
2. One preorder checkout (Stripe Checkout hosted — no SKU complexity).
3. One email capture (waitlist without paying).
4. One thank-you page.

Everything else is Phase 2.
